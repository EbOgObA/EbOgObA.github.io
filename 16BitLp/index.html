Rules & Demo Game
  • Very simple intuitive interface
  • Demo Game is the same as the real game
  • You can try your luck just for fun

Try your luck in a realistic demo game. Smart contract, rules and chances to win are the same as in a real game!

Players are invited to place bets on the playing field of 24 cells (16 numbers and 8 letters). If you win, the bet is multiplied by the corresponding coefficient. There is a draw every minute.

The cells are highlighted in different colors, so you can see both your bets and winnings, as well as all other participants’

x15 – A winning bet on a number from 0 to 15 is multiplied by 15 times

х5 - A bet on sectors ABCDE covers three numbers: in case of a win, the bet is multiplied by 5 times

х3 - A bet on XYZ sectors covers five numbers: in case of a win, the bet is multiplied by 3 times

If a cell is not active and it is not possible to bet on it, then the maximum value of the sum of bets has been reached.
