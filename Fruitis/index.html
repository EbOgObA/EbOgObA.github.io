About FreshPlanet

FreshPlanet started in 2012 as an organic supply company, starting with a small installation and a great job behind it. Today we have one of the most advanced lines in the World. Quality and cutting-edge technology are the key factors that drive us to export to the China, North Africa, Europe and the rest of the world to stand out in our work.

We pioneered the short supply chain so you can experience fresh food at its finest. Food comes to us straight from the source and is delivered to your door at peak freshness in just a few days. That’s less handling by middlemen—and it puts more money in the bank for local farmers and skilled artisans.
